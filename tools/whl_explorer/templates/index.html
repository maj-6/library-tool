          Integrations
          
            
          Google Sheets
          
            
          Publishes the master list (plus manual entries) to a Google
              Sheet — always triggered manually (File > Sync master list to Google Sheets).
              Requires a Google service-account JSON key; not verified yet (no credentials).
          
            
          
              
              
              
              
            
          

            
          Search databases
          
            
          Search is local-first: a database already in the
              database folder (shown below, in your home directory) is used offline — no download,
              no URL. Have copies (say, on a flash drive)? Open the folder, drop them in, and reopen
              the app. A source URL is only needed to download a database you don't already
              have; without one, queries fall back to the cloud search URL below.
          
            
          
              
              
            
          
            
          
            
          
              
              
            
          
            
          
              
              
            
          

            
          Phone capture (Supabase)
          
            
          Phone photos land here; syncing runs the pipeline and files
              each capture as a manual entry. Sign in on the phone and desktop; the same account
              works automatically, and linked contributor accounts are also supported. The
              built-in public cloud configuration handles the rest, with no Supabase key to
              enter. The Mistral key it needs lives under Credentials.
          
            
          
              
              
              
              
              
              
              
              
              
              
              
              
            
          
            
          
              
              
            
          
          
          
          
          
            
          Credentials
          
            
          Optional service credentials. A Library Tool account
              uses the built-in public cloud configuration and never needs an owner key.
              Mistral follows your signed-in profile; other provider keys stay on this machine.

        Welcome
        
      
          
      
      
          

        
          
          
          Welcome to Library Tool.
          
          
          Catalog books, compare them with library records, review copyright and
             source availability, and turn verified scans into readable volumes.
          
          
            
            
          • Catalogs is where you search, compare, and verify books.
            • 
            
          • Workbench moves each book through Record, Source, Text, Knowledge, and Publish.
            • 
            
          • Published Library previews what readers can browse online.
            • 
          
          
          
          Setup takes about a minute. Every choice is optional
             and remains available in Settings.
          
        
          

        
          
          
          Choose how your work is identified.
          
          
          A display name labels your local activity. Sign in to use
             phone capture and share contributor activity. Local cataloging does not require
             an account.
